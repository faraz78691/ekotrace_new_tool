Franchises

Franchise

Emissions from the operations of franchises owned by the company who grants the license to other entities to sell or distribute its product or service. This includes all emissions resulting from the franchise operations, such as energy use, transportation, and waste.

To collect emissions data for Franchise used by the company, follow these steps:
  1. Identify all potential sources of emissions within the franchise (e.g., electricity usage, heating, vehicle fleets).
  2. Gather utility bills or usage records for each franchise location.
  3. Fuel Usage: Record fuel consumption for any company-owned vehicles and equipment.
  4. Waste Management: Track waste generation and disposal methods, including recycling and landfill use.
  5. Other Sources: Identify any additional sources of emissions specific to your franchise operations (e.g., industrial processes).
How to use the tool for entering data emissions
  1. Select the franchise asset type and the sub-category from the dropdown.
  2. Select the calculation method.
    • If selected ‘Average data method’, enter the area of the franchise in sq meter or sq. feet.
    • If selected ‘Investment specific method’, download the template to collect Scope1 and Scope2 emissions data associated with using the franchise for the reporting period
