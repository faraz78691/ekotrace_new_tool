Fuel and Energy-related activities

Emissions related to the production (cradle to gate) of fuels and energy purchased and consumed by the company in the reporting year. It excludes emissions from the combustion of fuels and electricity consumed by the company as they are already included in scope 1 or scope 2.

How to use the tool for entering data emissions

Emissions data for this section is taken from Scope1 - Stationary Combustion and Scope 2 - Electricity and Heat & Steam categories. Cradle to Gate emission factors are then used for fuel data and transmission loss factor for the electricity data to calculate emissions for this category

Emisisons data for this section is taken from Scope1 - Stationary Combustion and Scope 2 - Electricity and Heat & Steam categories. Craddle to Gate emission factors are then used to compute Scope3.
